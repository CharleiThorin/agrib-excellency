  • Our Beginnings

    While working with Agricultural producers, researchers and financial Institutions, We Identified a gap in reliance on Agricultural Innovations and technologies to Increase Agricultural Productivity in the country. As a result of these inadequacies, a group of expert consultants came together and AGRIB EXCELLENCY LIMITED was founded.
    REASON : To bridge the gap in the use of Agricultural technologies and innovations to enhance agricultural competitiveness, and to also provide supporting services in order to promote growth of the sector.

  • Our Organization

    We are committed to agricultural development, financing and exposure of agricultural producers to new technologies and innovations.


    Our Vision

    Enabling Agricultural Development and Transformation.


    Our Mission

    To promote awareness and exposure about agricultural innovations,trends and supporting services to developing Agricultural projects.

  • Added 200+ Personnel including consultants, Researchers and Scientists

    We are made up of a network of agricultural producers, consultants, researchers and NGOs that, based on their area of interest in a specified Agricultural field provide/receive resources to further their endeavours.
